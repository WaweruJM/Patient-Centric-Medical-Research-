Patient-Centric Medical Research 

This repository presents an academic resource on
Patient-Centric Medical Research (PACEMERE) Principles.

The PACEMERE framework emphasizes the integration of ethical
responsibility, methodological rigor, patient involvement, translational
relevance, and health equity in contemporary medical research.

It aims at assisting anyone considering medical research, and looking for new dynamics in current medical research designs.

-   Medical researchers
-   Postgraduate students
-   Academic institutions
-   Health policy scholars
-   Global health stakeholders
